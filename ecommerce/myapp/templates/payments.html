Payment Policy

Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfers
  • Other payment methods as may be offered at checkout

Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely using SSL encryption to ensure that your information is protected. We do not store your payment information on our servers.

Payment Confirmation

After placing an order, you will receive a payment confirmation email detailing the transaction. This email will serve as your receipt.

Billing

Your credit/debit card will be charged when your order is processed. If your card is declined, your order will not be processed and you will be notified to provide an alternative payment method.

Currency

All prices listed on our website are in dollars unless otherwise stated.

Taxes

All applicable taxes will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

Failed Transactions

If your payment fails, please try an alternative payment method or contact your bank for further assistance. You can also reach out to our customer support for help.

Refunds

For information on refunds, please refer to our Return Policy page.
